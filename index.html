Aeon Classic isn't just a currency. It's a lifestyle.

  • CPoW algorithm: CryptoNight-Lite v1
  • Max supply: ~36.8 million
  • Block reward: smoothly decreasing
  • Block time: 120 seconds
  • Difficulty: retargets at every block
  • Absolutely no premine or ICO, fairly launched.

    • A lightweight CryptoNote digital currency

    Privacy oriented with a mobile focus

    Decentralized digital currency is slowly becoming a normal part of everyday life. Yet everybody's main internet device continues to be their cellphone, a device with a low-powered CPU and limited available storage. Aeon Classic is about enabling this era, enabling an age where all people everywhere have the freedom to privately send and receive money with whatever gadget they already own.

    Secure

    Each transaction is secured with robust cryptography and distributed through a global peer-to-peer consensus network.

    Private

    By default, Aeon Classic utilizes a cryptographic system to transfer funds without the identifying information of each user becoming visible on the blockchain.

    Untraceable

    Aeon Classic uses ring signatures to make transactions untraceable, meaning it is very difficult for blockchain analysis to determine if funds have been spent.
